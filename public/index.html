About Us

The Retreat Whickham, was founded in May 2016, with the mission to provide the highest quality beauty experiences, natural products and holistic treatments in the beautiful setting of Whickham village. Since 2022, The Retreat Laser & Advanced Skin Clinic has used innovative technology to deliver lasting results, from permanent hair removal with the Alpha IPL system to firmer, clearer skin with Endymed 3DEEP radiofrequency treatments.
