What is the FaceStim Facial?

The FaceStim Facial is a non-invasive, results-driven treatment designed to lift, tone and sculpt the face while improving overall skin firmness and structure.

With no downtime, the FaceStim Facial is an effective non-surgical alternative for those looking to improve facial definition, lift and overall skin quality.

Benefits

  • Stimulates collagen and elastin production
  • Tones and sculpts facial contours
  • Lifts muscles non-invasively
  • Volumises and tightens the skin
  • Smooths and evens skin tone and texture
  • Reduces the appearance of fine lines

The Technology

Using FACESTIM® technology, this treatment combines bipolar radio frequency with deep muscle contraction to strengthen facial muscles while stimulating collagen production in the skin. This dual approach helps tighten the skin, improve tone and restore volume for a firmer, more lifted appearance.

Ideal For

Sagging or lax skin
Fine lines & early wrinkles
Loss of facial volume
