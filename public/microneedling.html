What is eDermastamp Microneedling?

Advanced, electronic, medical skin-needling that stimulates the skin to regenerate and repair itself naturally and safely. This technique creates smoother, brighter, younger and healthier-looking skin.

The precision engineering of the device enables your therapist to accurately control needle depth and speed by area and skin condition. Every session includes LED light therapy treatment to further enhance your results.

Benefits

  • Skin rejuvenation & collagen induction
  • Tissue regeneration & reduced pigmentation
  • Smaller pores & scar reduction
  • Fine line smoothing

Recommended: 3–4 treatments, 4 weeks apart.
